Hotel Receptionist Checklist

  • Check handovers from the previous shift
  • Count the float and inform the shift leader of any issues
  • Delete emails from Booking.com
  • Print registration cards
  • Flip the registration cards from departure rooms
  • Check balances and settle sales ledgers and virtual cards
  • Check traces for special requests from arriving guests
  • Check changes for Air Canada
  • After 12:00 PM, make a list of rooms due to check out and call the rooms
  • Go to rooms and check for luggage if there is no answer
  • Break time between 12:00 PM and 1:30 PM
  • Receptionist clocks out at 3:00 PM
  • Before clocking out, close the cashier
  • Count the float again and get a signature from the shift leader
  • Pass the handover to the next shift
